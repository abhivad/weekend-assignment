Smart KYC management system - AI based document scanning, Face recognition & Biometrics operations. 3. Identity based Smart Login and Authenticator system - Login systems like Singpass (Singapore), Bank one (Sweden), MyAadhar (India), MyGovID(Australia) 4. E-commerce application - Integrated payment through Fiat, Crypto or any Digital Currencies
